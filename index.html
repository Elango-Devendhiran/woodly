Our Story

Born in a
Backyard Workshop

In 2018, master carpenter Rajan Kumar started Woodly in his backyard workshop in Ooty, Tamil Nadu. Frustrated by plastic toys that broke in days, he crafted his daughter's first wooden puzzle — and she was enchanted.

Word spread. Parents wanted toys that were safe, durable, and beautiful. Today, Woodly employs 40 skilled artisans and ships to every corner of India.

Payment Gateways & Setup

Everything you need to know about accepting payments on your Woodly store — from choosing the right gateway to going live.

Recommended Payment Gateways

Razorpay

Best for Indian businesses. Accepts UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe), EMI. Instant settlement. No setup fee.

Essential for Tier-2/3 cities. No gateway fees, but adds ₹50–₹80 COD charge. Builds trust with first-time buyers who prefer not to pay online.

₹50 flat
Setup: Manual / logistics partner
Return rate: Higher than prepaid
Payout: On delivery

🚀 Razorpay Setup: Step-by-Step

1

Create a Razorpay Account

Go to razorpay.com → Sign Up. Enter your business email and mobile number. Verify OTP.

2

Complete KYC

Submit: PAN card, GST number (if applicable), Bank account details, Aadhaar/Passport, Business registration proof.

3

Get API Keys

After approval, go to Settings → API Keys → Generate Key. Copy your Key ID and Key Secret safely.

4

Integrate on Website

Add the Razorpay checkout script to your site. Pass order amount, currency, and your Key ID. Test in sandbox mode first.

5

Test Transactions

Use Razorpay's test card numbers to simulate payments. Verify webhook callbacks for order confirmation.

6

Go Live!

Switch from test keys to live keys. Enable SSL on your website. You're ready to accept real payments!

Shipping Partner

✅

Privacy & Return Policy

Required by payment gateways. Clearly state return window and refund process.
